About RentACar

We've been your trusted choice for premium car rentals since 2010

Our Story

RentACar was founded in 2010 with a clear goal: to make car rental simple for everyone. We wanted to make renting a car easy, affordable, and enjoyable. What began as a small operation with just 10 vehicles has grown into one of the most trusted names in car rentals today.

Today, we serve customers in more than 25 locations with a fleet of over 500 vehicles. Our vehicles range from economy cars to luxury SUVs. We have built our success on exceptional customer service, transparent pricing, and a strong commitment to quality. Our commitment to quality never changes.

We think driving should give you a sense of freedom and possibility. Maybe you're planning a family road trip, or need a reliable car for business travel, or want to enjoy the thrill of driving a luxury car. We're here to help you do just that.
